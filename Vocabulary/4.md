### New Phrases

Thai:
Q: kin khâaw rʉ́yaŋ (eat rice yet)
(+) kin lɛɛw (eat already)
(-) yaŋ mâi kin (yet not eating)
English:
Q: Have you eaten yet?
A: +/-

---

Thai:
Q: pen yaŋŋay bâaŋ (to be how some)
(+): sa baay dii (kh.)
(-): mâi sa baay (kh.)
English:
Q: How are you doing?
A: +/-

---

Thai:
Q: pay nǎy maa (go where come)
A: pay ___ maa (kh.)
English:
Q: Where have you been?
A: I've been to ___
